Dr. Muhammad Farooq Naeemi Al-Bukhari

is an Islamic Scholar, Leader, Educator and Activist who envisioned the idea of providing educational and humanitarian aid for the development of the Muslim minority in India. Taking charge of the legacy gifted by the Muslim scholarship in India, he came out as a vibrant teacher for millions and an empathizer for the needy.
